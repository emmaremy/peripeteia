Welcome!

Peripeteia 2018 is happening this weekend, January 26-28! View the schedule of events here.

Peripeteia is an event where anyone can teach a course on anything they like, academic or non-academic. Learn about past Peripeteia weekends here. We also host panel discussions called Preludes.

The Peripeteia Planning Committee also manages a weekly digest of departmental events called PILE. So if you're a math major who wants to go to an art history talk now and then, this list can help remind you when and where they are. You can sign up to receive PILE's weekly emails here.

You can learn more about Peripeteia or PILE by contacting swatperipeteia@gmail.com, on our about page, or by contacting any of the members of the Peripeteia planning committee.
